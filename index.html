 Hackathon Rules

…With the First edition of our Hackathon coming soon, we wanted to introduce you to our rules for participation.

  • Hackathon is open for all college students.
  • Participants of teams can be from any year of study.
  • Teams must comprise 1-4 people.
  • Participants are prohibited to copy projects from any online resource.
  • Selected candidates will need to present their projects to the panel of judges.
  • Any work done out of the duration of the hackathon must be declared to your Person of Contact.
  • Keep checking your email and discord server for regular updates.
